# CCMQ Question Reduction Analysis (60 to 30 Items)

## Objective
To reduce the 60-item Constitution in Chinese Medicine Questionnaire (CCMQ) to a validated 30-item "Lite" version (SF-CCMQ) suitable for a mobile app. This reduction prioritizes high factor loadings, eliminates redundancies, and ensures at least 3 items per constitution for statistical reliability in Latent Class Analysis (LCA).

## Categorization & Redundancy Elimination

### 1. Temperature Sensitivity
*   **Original Items:** Q16, Q17, Q18, Q19, Q20, Q21, Q37, Q51, Q54
*   **Overlaps & Proxies:**
    *   *Yang-Deficiency (Cold):* Q17 (Cold hands/feet), Q18 (Cold abdomen/back), Q19 (Sensitive to cold), and Q21 (Vulnerable to cold) all measure cold intolerance. **Action:** Retain **Q17** as the primary proxy for peripheral coldness, and **Q51/Q54** for digestive cold intolerance.
    *   *Yin-Deficiency (Heat):* Q16 (Hot palms/soles), Q20 (Hot body/face), and Q37 (Hot flashes) all measure false heat. **Action:** Retain **Q16** as the classic clinical indicator for Yin-Deficiency heat.

### 2. Respiratory & Energy Levels
*   **Original Items:** Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4, Q5, Q6, Q7, Q13, Q14, Q22, Q26
*   **Overlaps & Proxies:**
    *   *Qi-Deficiency (Fatigue):* Q2 (Tired easily), Q6 (Dislike talking), and Q7 (Weak voice) all measure low energy. **Action:** Retain **Q2** as the strongest general indicator. Retain **Q3** (Shortness of breath) and **Q26** (Sweating easily) for specific Qi-sinking symptoms.

### 3. Digestion & Excretion
*   **Original Items:** Q47, Q48, Q51, Q54, Q55, Q56, Q57, Q58
*   **Overlaps & Proxies:**
    *   These items are highly specific to different constitutions and have low overlap. 
    *   **Action:** Retain distinct indicators: **Q55** (Sticky stools -> Damp-Heat), **Q56** (Dry stools -> Yin-Deficiency), **Q57** (Flabby stomach -> Phlegm-Dampness), and **Q47** (Bitter taste -> Damp-Heat).

### 4. Skin, Tongue & Face
*   **Original Items:** Q28, Q29, Q31, Q32, Q33, Q34, Q35, Q38, Q39, Q40, Q41, Q42, Q44, Q49
*   **Overlaps & Proxies:**
    *   *Dampness (Oily):* Q28 (Oily T-zone) and Q38 (Greasy face). **Action:** Merge into **Q38**.
    *   *Blood-Stasis (Dark/Purple):* Q33 (Bruises), Q35 (Capillary veins), Q39 (Dark complexion), Q42 (Dark circles), Q44 (Dark lips). **Action:** Retain **Q33** (Bruises) and **Q44** (Dark lips) as the most reliable visual indicators.
    *   *Phlegm-Dampness:* Retain **Q49** (Thick tongue coating).

### 5. Mental State & Sleep
*   **Original Items:** Q8, Q9, Q10, Q11, Q27, Q53
*   **Overlaps & Proxies:**
    *   *Qi-Stagnation (Mood):* Q8 (Gloomy), Q9 (Anxious), Q10 (Sentimental), Q11 (Frightened). **Action:** Retain **Q8** and **Q9** as the primary emotional markers, along with **Q14** (Sighing) and **Q53** (Insomnia).

---

## The Final 30-Item Lite Questionnaire (SF-CCMQ)
*Maintains 3-4 items per constitution.*

1. **Gentleness (3):** Q1 (Energetic), Q27 (Forgetful - Reverse), Q52 (Adaptable)
2. **Qi-Deficiency (4):** Q2 (Tired), Q3 (Short breath), Q22 (Catch colds), Q26 (Sweat easily)
3. **Yang-Deficiency (3):** Q17 (Cold hands/feet), Q51 (Uncomfortable after cold food), Q54 (Diarrhea from cold)
4. **Yin-Deficiency (3):** Q16 (Hot palms/soles), Q29 (Dry skin/lips), Q45 (Parched/thirsty)
5. **Phlegm-Dampness (3):** Q15 (Heavy body), Q49 (Thick tongue coating), Q57 (Flabby stomach)
6. **Damp-Heat (4):** Q38 (Greasy face), Q40 (Acne/sores), Q47 (Bitter taste), Q55 (Sticky stools)
7. **Blood-Stasis (3):** Q33 (Bruises), Q36 (Body pain), Q44 (Dark lips)
8. **Qi-Stagnation (4):** Q8 (Gloomy), Q9 (Anxious), Q14 (Sighing), Q53 (Insomnia)
9. **Special-Diathesis (3):** Q23 (Sneeze without cold), Q30 (Allergies), Q31 (Hives)
